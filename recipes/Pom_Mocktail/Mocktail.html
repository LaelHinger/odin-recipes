Description

This pomegranate mocktail, with lime juice and grapefruit lime tepache soda, is refreshing and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 6 basil leaves, plus more for garnish
  • 1 fluid ounce lime juice
  • 4 fluid ounces pomegranate juice
  • 1 fluid ounce grapefruit lime flavor tepache soda (such as De La CalleÂ® Tepache)

Steps

  1. Place basil and lime juice into a cocktail shaker and mash well with a cocktail muddler. Pour in pomegranate juice; cover and shake well. Strain into a glass with ice, top off with tepache soda, and garnish with freshly torn basil leaves.
