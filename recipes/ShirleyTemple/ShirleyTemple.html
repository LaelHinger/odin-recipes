This pomegranate mocktail, with lime juice and grapefruit lime tepache soda, is refreshing and flavorful.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup orange juice
  • ½ cup lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
  • 1 tablespoon grenadine syrup
  • 1 maraschino cherry

Steps

  1. Combine orange juice and lemon-lime soda in a tall glass. Pour grenadine in and let it sink to the bottom. Garnish with a maraschino cherry and a straw for stirring.
